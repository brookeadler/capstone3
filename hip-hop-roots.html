HIP HOP ROOTS

Hip Hop is undoubtedly the most popular musical genre today. While new songs and artists are unique to our time, many of them have their roots in the charismatic, soulful sound of Motown. Current hip-hop artists sample earlier songs by replicating their rhythms, beats, and lyrics. The art of sampling is inseparable with hip-hop, and the Black influence on contemporary music. Hip-Hop artists today recycle Motown songs in their work in order to produce unique and meaningful songs.
