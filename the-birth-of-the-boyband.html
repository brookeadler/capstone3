THE BIRTH OF THE BOY BAND

Motown artists defined the modern idea of the boy band. Groups such as The Jackson Five, The Temptations, and the Four Tops entertained the masses. Their music combined R&B, Jazz, Rock & Roll, Soul, and Gospel to produce an authentic Motown sound. Their effortless harmonies, call and response song patterns, and romantic lyrics swooned audiences across America, including people of all races.
