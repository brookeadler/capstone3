MUSIC AS RELIGION

The music of Motown has clear roots in the church. Many artists began their careers as members of their local choirs. The distinct call and response pattern of Motown songs originated in gospel hymns and tunes. Today, contemporary music is widely influenced by religion. Many famous artists such as Whitney Houston and John Legend learned how to sing in the church. Beloved rappers such as Kanye West and Chance the Rapper have clearly been influenced by christianity and the gospel roots of Motown.
