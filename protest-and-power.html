PROTEST AND POWER

The early sixties and seventies were undoubtedly a time of civil unrest, protest, and anger. From the assassination of Martin Luther King Jr., to the Vietnam War, the artists of Motown used their platforms to speak out on greater issues. The tradition of the protest song is extremely relevant today. from Beyoncé’s Black Parade, to the passionate lyrics of Kendrick Lamar, protest songs will never lose their fervor and resonance.
